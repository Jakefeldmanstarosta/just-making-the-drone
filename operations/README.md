# Operations

Internal processes, vendors, facilities, and the operational backbone of the company.

## What lives here

- `processes/` — Standard operating procedures (SOPs): how we order parts, how we onboard a new vendor, how we run a release.
- `vendors/` — Master vendor list across the company (engineering suppliers live in each product version's `docs/suppliers.md`, but vendor *contracts* and master data live here).
- `facilities/` — Workspace, equipment inventory, lab safety.
- `meetings/` — Recurring meeting agendas and notes.
