# Marketing

Brand, website, content, launches, and external storytelling.

## What lives here

- `brand/` — Logo files, color palette, typography, voice & tone guide.
- `website/` — Source for the company website (or links to where it's hosted).
- `content/` — Blog posts, social posts, newsletters, video scripts.
- `launches/` — Plans, checklists, and post-mortems for each product launch.
- `press/` — Press kit, press releases, media contacts.

## Workflow

1. Draft content in `content/drafts/`.
2. Review with relevant lead (engineering for technical claims, legal for anything regulated).
3. Move to `content/published/` once live, with a link to where it was published.

## Launch checklist (template)

- [ ] Product page / landing page ready
- [ ] Press release drafted and approved
- [ ] Social posts queued
- [ ] Email to mailing list scheduled
- [ ] Engineering and legal review complete
- [ ] Customer support briefed
