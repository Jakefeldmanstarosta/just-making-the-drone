# Human Resources

Hiring, onboarding, policies, org chart, and the people side of the company.

## What lives here

- `hiring/` — Open roles, job descriptions, interview rubrics, candidate pipeline.
- `onboarding/` — New-hire checklist, welcome packet, training materials.
- `policies/` — Employee handbook, time off policy, code of conduct.
- `org/` — Org chart, reporting lines, team rosters.
- `reviews/` — Performance review templates and cadence.

## Don't commit

- Salaries, comp bands, personal employee data — store those in an access-controlled HR system
- Anything from a candidate they wouldn't expect us to share publicly
- Disciplinary records

## Onboarding a new hire (template)

- [ ] Sign offer letter (`../legal/contracts/templates/`)
- [ ] Set up email + accounts
- [ ] Add to org chart
- [ ] Schedule intro with team leads
- [ ] Walk through this repo (start with the top-level `README.md`)
- [ ] Assign first-week buddy
